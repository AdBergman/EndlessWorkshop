Endless Legend 2 Unit Evolution Explorer

Endless Workshop provides an Endless Legend 2 unit explorer that lets players inspect faction rosters, compare evolution paths, and review linked unit progression.

What the explorer covers

  • Browse major-faction unit lines and their evolution branches.
  • Switch into minor-faction mode to review currently surfaced minor units.
  • Use shareable route parameters to jump directly to a specific faction and unit.

Interactive data loading

The live explorer loads unit data after the app starts, which keeps roster details and evolution links aligned with the current Endless Workshop dataset.
